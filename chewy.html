Project Overview

Project Background

This mini project was a requirement for my Digital Marketing Fellowship at COOP Careers. I worked in a small team with two other fellows to analyze and conduct a marketing performance report for 2024 Quarter 1 and Quarter 2 for Chewy.

My Contribution

My responsibility as Quality Assurance was to ensure that data was accurate and properly sorted using VLOOKUPs and Pivot Tables. This role helped maintain data integrity and accuracy for the final marketing performance report.

Marketing Analysis Summary

This is a quick overview of the Chewy performance marketing. Please view the presentation in the next section for a deeper analysis or download the Excel sheet.

  • Overall performance breakdown per channel, including Orders, Revenue, CPC, CPA, CTR, and CVR.
  • Identification of low- and high-performing campaigns.
  • Quarterly cost efficiency analysis and identification of the highest efficiency quarter.
  • Overview of combined SEM and Paid Social channels.
  • Highlighting the most efficient channel and campaign in terms of CTR, CVR, and CPC.
  • Detailed Fish Campaign performance for ads on Social Media and SEM, with recommendations to reallocate budget if not cost efficient.
  • Reptile Campaign performance for ads on Social Media (Meta), noting inactivity on SEM.
  • Additional insights as needed, provided on a weekly and monthly basis.
  • Visuals per channel and campaign, including Clicks, Orders, and Revenue.
  • SEM - The highest-performing campaign for Q1 and Q2 is the Dog campaign with the highest-grossing revenue, while the Fish campaign had the highest-performing CVR at 11.37% for Q1 and Q2.
  • Meta Awareness - Invested approximately $300k on clicks for the Cat campaign, but it had the lowest clicks on ads.
  • Meta Retarget - Fish ads have a CTR of 0.44%, meaning there are potential issues with customer engagement.

During this project, I learned the importance of data accuracy and attention to detail in driving successful marketing outcomes. I also discovered the value of telling a compelling story based on the data gathered from key performance indicators (KPIs). What made the project successful was my ability to use Excel tools such as pivot tables, VLOOKUPs, and conditional formatting to visualize the data in a clear and accessible way. This made it easier to distinguish between high-performing and underperforming campaigns.
